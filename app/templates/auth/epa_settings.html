About Statbotics EPA

EPA (Expected Points Added) is a metric from Statbotics that estimates how many points a team contributes to their alliance per match.

EPA is broken into Auto, Teleop, and Endgame components and is updated continuously throughout the season.

Tip: Use "Scouted + Gap-Fill" mode for the best of both worlds — your scouts' detailed data for teams you've watched, plus Statbotics data for teams you haven't scouted yet.
