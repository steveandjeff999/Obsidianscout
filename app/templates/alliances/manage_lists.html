How these lists work: {{ help.help_icon('Explanation of Avoid, Do-not-pick, Declined, and Want lists and how they affect recommendations.', 'How lists work') }}
  • Team Tags: Offense/Defense tags are managed in a separate list and can be prioritized in both points and custom-rank sorting.
  • Custom Pick List: Set your own ranked pick order and optional short notes. Drag to reorder.
  • Avoid List: Teams will still appear in recommendations but with a 30% reduction in their ranking score
  • Do Not Pick List: Teams will only be recommended if there are no other available teams
  • Declined List: Teams are hidden from recommendations but can be manually assigned
  • All lists only affect recommendations - you can still manually assign any team to an alliance
