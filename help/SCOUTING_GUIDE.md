# Scouting Guide

This guide covers the standard match-scouting workflow.

## Before matches

- Log in with a scout or analytics account
- Confirm the event and match schedule are loaded
- Confirm your scout assignment (which team/position you watch)

## During each match

1. Open a new scouting entry
2. Select event, match, and team
3. Record actions as they happen (auto, teleop, endgame, notes)
4. Submit immediately after the match
5. Confirm the saved record appears in the scouting list or QR/export workflow before moving to the next match

## Data quality rules

- Record only what you observed
- Keep notes short and factual
- Double-check team number before submit
- Stay consistent with rating scales across matches

## QR workflow (optional)

Use QR tools when devices are offline or partially connected:

- Generate QR after submission on source device
- Scan/import QR on destination device
- Confirm entry appears in scouting list

## Offline guidance

- Continue entering data locally when connection drops
- Reconnect and allow sync to finish
- If sync fails, use QR transfer as backup

## Quick checks if something looks wrong

- Refresh the page and confirm you are still logged in
- Verify required fields are filled
- Check team/event selection before re-submitting

For deeper fixes, see `TROUBLESHOOTING.md`.
